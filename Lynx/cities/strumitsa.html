Strumitsa

Strumica, in southeastern North Macedonia, is a vibrant city steeped in history and culture. Known for its archaeological sites and the famous Strumica Carnival, it offers visitors a blend of tradition and modernity. With its lively atmosphere and rich heritage, Strumica is a must-visit destination for those exploring North Macedonia.
