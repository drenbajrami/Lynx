Veles

Veles, located in the central part of North Macedonia along the Vardar River, is a city rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. With a population of around 58,000 people, it serves as an important economic and cultural center in the region. Veles has a long and storied past, with archaeological evidence suggesting settlement dating back to ancient times, including Roman and Byzantine periods.
