Struga

Struga, nestled on Lake Ohrid's shores in southwestern North Macedonia, is famed for its natural beauty and cultural significance. Known as the "City of Poetry" for hosting the prestigious Struga Poetry Evenings, it attracts artists and visitors alike. With a scenic backdrop of the lake and mountains, Struga offers a tranquil setting for exploration and relaxation, making it a cherished destination for those seeking both inspiration and natural charm.
