Prilep

Prilep, situated centrally in North Macedonia, is a city rich in history and culture. Known as the "City of Marko's Towers," it carries a legacy dating back to antiquity, with landmarks like the medieval fortress of King Marko and the Monastery of Treskavec. Renowned for its tobacco industry and traditional crafts, Prilep is both an economic hub and a cultural treasure, offering visitors a compelling blend of historical intrigue and contemporary charm.
