Ohrid

Ohrid, in southwestern North Macedonia, is celebrated for its historical allure and natural splendor. As the "Pearl of the Balkans," it entices with its UNESCO-designated old town, ancient churches, and the tranquil Ohrid Lake. With a rich past dating back millennia, including landmarks like the Ohrid Fortress, the city's cobblestone streets offer a delightful blend of culture and scenic beauty, making it a top destination for history buffs and nature lovers alike.
