Skopje

Skopje, the capital of North Macedonia, is a dynamic blend of history and modernity. Set along the Vardar River, it boasts diverse architecture, from ancient Ottoman structures to contemporary monuments. Landmarks like Kale Fortress and the Stone Bridge dot the cityscape, while vibrant cafes and cultural venues contribute to its lively atmosphere. As the country's political, economic, and cultural hub, Skopje embodies the essence of North Macedonia, offering visitors a rich tapestry of experiences.
