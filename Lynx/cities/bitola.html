Bitola

Bitola, in southwestern North Macedonia, is a city steeped in history and cultural heritage. Its streets echo tales of Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman influence, evident in its mosques and bustling bazaars. Home to Bitola National Theatre and prestigious universities, it's a vibrant intellectual center. Surrounded by the stunning landscapes of the Baba Mountain range and Pelister National Park, Bitola offers both historical intrigue and natural beauty, attracting visitors from far and wide.
